Our Philosophy

Craft & Care at
Every Chair

Founded in 2009 on Stratford High Street, Da Cuts Salon was built on one belief: everyone deserves to leave feeling genuinely transformed. For over fifteen years we have been East London's trusted destination for hair and beauty.

Our certified stylists bring international training to your local high street — whether you want a precision cut, bold colour, or a relaxing treatment, we take the time to understand exactly what you need.

  • ✦ Wella & Schwarzkopf Certified
  • ✦ Award-winning stylists
  • ✦ Free colour consultations
  • ✦ No hidden charges, ever
